Chicken Enchiladas

Chicken Enchiladas is an easy to make, yummy to eat, mexican dish that comes together for an easy dinner.

ingredients

  • 1 TBS olive oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 3/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/2 pint sour cream
  • 1 TBS dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 (15oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/3 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 TBS chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water (optional)
  • 8 (10in) flour tortillas
  • 1 (12oz) jar taco sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink
    and juices run clear, 5-7 minutes per side
  3. Transfer chicken to a cutting board and slice into cubes. Return chicken to the skillet
  4. Add onion, 1 cup Cheddar cheese, sour cream, parsley, oregano, and black pepper.
    Cook and stir over low heat until cheese melts. Stir in tomato sauce, green pepper,
    garlic, chili powder, and salt. Add water if needed.
  5. Spoon chicken mixture into tortillas and roll into enchiladas. Arrange enchiladas,
    seam-side down, in a 9x13in baking dish. Top with taco sauce and remaining 3/4 cup
    Cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven, uncovered, until cheese has melted, about 20 minutes.
    Let cool breifly before serving.
