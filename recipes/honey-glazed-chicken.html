Honey Glazed Chicken

With just 5 ingredients this dinner is incredibly easy to make while still being full of delicious flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2TBS soy sauce
  • 1/8tps red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2TBS olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
